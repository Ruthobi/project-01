About us

Since 2020, we have been strongly committed to providing our customers with the highest level of service in the building and construction markets. Building and maintaining relationships with our customers is at the heart of our business and we have a planning and management style that is consultative and systematic. Because we employ our own qualified tradesmen and apprentices we are better able to maintain high standards of quality, safety and delivery.

We give our customers confidence and peace of mind during the process of planning, design and construction. The hands-on responsibilities and team approach of company directors Michael Peters, Martin Stephens, John Nwado and David Joyce underpin our organisation with a powerful and proven synergy.

Construction Manager

Construction manager specialises in the management of construction projects, and are often hired by businesses or organisations that do not have the internal staff.

Construction Laborer

Construction labourers are unskilled workers and perform tasks such as digging trenches, mixing concrete, hauling materials, and performing general cleanup.

Construction Services

UpConstruction provides new construction, interior remodels, exterior remodels and building maintenance in the public and private sectors. Areas of work include retail, offices, financial institutions, transportation sites, educational facilities, government properties, warehouses, and shipping facilities. Our in-house work includes but is not limited to sidewalks, concrete pads and ramps, framing, drywall, acoustical ceilings, wood trim work, doors, frames, hardware, and pallet racking systems.
